## Introducing Alchemize

Multiple Seasons Multiple Themes and a Single Dynamic Shop

Fully community driven 

**YOU SHIP:** 
- Ship any project matching any theme of the running season
- Earn points of that theme
- Convert them to the *Potion Mix*

**WE SHIP:**
- A dynamic shop with a variety of items that can be bought with the *Potion Mix*
- New Items are added every week, each and every item is suggested by the community.

**THE PLOT TWIST**
- Each season lasts only 20 days, tho the *Potion Mix* persists, so you can use *Potion Mix* earned in one season to buy items in the next season, and so on.
- Each season has a different theme, and the themes are decided by the community as well.
- the 3 themes would have different difficulty levels, thus seasons with harder themes would reward more points, and thus more *Potion Mix*.
- When converting theme points to *Potion Mix*, you can choose to convert all your points seperately and recieve normal amount OR convert them together and get a bonus, the more points you convert together the bigger the bonus.
